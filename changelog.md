# Changelog

All notable changes to **Exciting Adventures** are documented here.
The format is based on [Keep a Changelog](https://keepachangelog.com/).
For the complete per-file release history, see the
[CurseForge files page](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/modpacks/exciting-adventures/files).

## [Unreleased]

### Added
- **Chronicles of the Abyss** — the narrative campaign expanded beyond the quest book:
  - An in-world story engine — a guide codex and a starting letter on first join, and
    atmospheric narration when you reach the Deep Dark, the Nether, the End and the great bosses.
  - Story milestones surfaced as custom advancements in a dedicated *Chronicles of the Abyss* tab.
  - **NPCs with branching dialogue** (the Hermit of the Shore, the Herald of the Abyss).
  - **A branching finale** — your choice (seal vs. embrace the Abyss) leads to two distinct endings.
  - Boss-fight music cues and an atmospheric resource pack (custom paintings).
- A reworked, themed main menu.

### Changed
- Hardened the FTB suite and performance mod set for stability.

### Fixed
- Quest-book text encoding (Cyrillic display).
- Removed/patched add-ons that were incompatible with the updated FTB suite.
